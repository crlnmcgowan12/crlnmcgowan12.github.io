Website Evaluations

Stripe.com

Stripe is a modern payment platform with a clean layout, targeting developers and businesses. It emphasizes clarity and usability.

  • Contrast: Headings and call-to-action buttons stand out; some text-over-image areas are borderline in readability.
  • Repetition: Typography, button styles, and spacing patterns are consistent across pages.
  • Alignment: Content aligns to a clean grid; margins and columns are consistent.
  • Proximity: Related items are grouped together; distinct sections have clear spacing.

Ling’s Cars

Ling’s Cars is a notoriously chaotic site with excessive colors, animations, and clutter, often considered difficult to navigate.

  • Contrast: Overdone with clashing neon colors; no clear focal point.
  • Repetition: Fonts, buttons, and styles vary wildly across sections.
  • Alignment: Elements are misaligned and floating randomly, giving a messy look.
  • Proximity: Items are overcrowded or spaced inconsistently; related content isn’t always grouped.
